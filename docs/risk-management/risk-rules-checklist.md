# Trading Risk Rules Checklist

Print this. Tape it to your monitor. Follow it every single trade.

---

## PRE-TRADE CHECKLIST

Read these BEFORE clicking buy/sell:

- [ ] **Is this a high-quality setup?** Don't force trades. No setup = no trade.
- [ ] **Have I calculated position size?** Use the sizing formula. No mental math.
- [ ] **Is my stop at a logical level?** Structure-based (below FVG, below swing low), not arbitrary.
- [ ] **Is risk:reward at least 1:2?** If not, skip the trade.
- [ ] **Am I within my daily loss budget?** Check running P&L before entry.
- [ ] **Am I within max open positions?** Options: max 3-4. Futures: max 3.
- [ ] **Is a bracket order attached?** Stop AND target must be live before you walk away.
- [ ] **Am I trading on emotion?** Revenge trade? FOMO? Boredom? → DON'T TRADE.

---

## DURING-TRADE RULES

Follow these while a trade is open:

- [ ] **NEVER remove or widen the stop.** This is non-negotiable. The stop is there to protect you.
- [ ] **Take partial profit at T1.** Close half at first target. Move stop to breakeven.
- [ ] **Let the rest trail.** Don't close early out of fear. Trust the bracket.
- [ ] **If uncertain, close half.** Don't hold full size through doubt. Reducing is fine.
- [ ] **Don't stare at the screen.** Set the bracket and walk away. Watching causes panic exits.
- [ ] **Never average down.** NEVER add to a losing position. Ever.

---

## POST-TRADE / END OF DAY

Do this after every trade and at the end of each session:

- [ ] **Are all positions closed?** Especially for prop firms — verify flat.
- [ ] **Did I hit my daily loss limit?** → DONE for the day. Close the platform.
- [ ] **Did I have 2+ consecutive losses?** → DONE for the day. Close the platform.
- [ ] **Journal entry completed?**
  - Screenshot of chart with entry/exit marked
  - Setup type (what pattern?)
  - Execution quality (1-5): Did I follow my rules?
  - What would I do differently?

## THE 10 COMMANDMENTS

1. **Every trade has a stop.** No exceptions. Bracket order = mandatory.
2. **Never move a stop further away.** If it's wrong, take the loss.
3. **Risk max 1-2% per trade.** Small losses are recoverable. Big losses aren't.
4. **Take partial profit at T1.** Locking in profit changes the psychology.
5. **2 consecutive losses = done for the day.** Walk away. Come back tomorrow.
6. **No revenge trading.** A loss is not a reason to trade more.
7. **No averaging down.** Adding to losers is how accounts blow up.
8. **Size down after drawdown.** Losing? Trade smaller, not bigger.
9. **Quality over quantity.** 1-3 great trades beat 10 mediocre ones.
10. **Journal every trade.** You can't improve what you don't measure.

## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

### If you catch yourself moving a stop:
1. Close the position immediately at market
2. Walk away from the computer for 30 minutes
3. Journal why you felt the urge
4. Done trading for the day

### If you hit daily loss limit:
1. Close all positions
2. Close the trading platform
3. Do NOT re-open it today
4. Review what went wrong in the evening

### If you're on a losing streak (3+ days):
1. Switch to micro contracts (MES/MNQ) or reduce to 1 option contract
2. Focus on execution quality, not P&L
3. Paper trade for 1-2 days if needed
4. Only return to full size after 2 consecutive green days

### If you're in drawdown (>50% of prop firm limit):
1. Switch to MES/MNQ micro contracts only
2. Target $50-100/day to rebuild slowly
3. If drawdown exceeds 70%, stop trading entirely
4. Consider whether the strategy or execution is the problem
